What is ABP?

ABP Network is essentially a TV channel that is driven by decisions individuals undertake on the blockchain. Traditional TV networks are centralized and decide what content they wish to broadcast, but with ABP content will be proposed by prospective producers and voted on by a governance system using masternodes. This will entrust the consumers and viewers to have power over the content they want to see produced and aired on the channel, while a governance fund will be attributed to the selected proposals.
